## Architectural Principles (The "Lego" Framework) Standardized Skeleton

Foundations must be rigid and follow company-proven patterns (.NET, REST, SQL, established NuGet/npm feeds). Modular Joints: Use Spec-Driven Development (SDD) to define immutable interfaces. 
### Lego Slices:
Features should be built as independent Lego pieces. As long as they fit the standardized "joints" (contracts), they can be developed in parallel without falling into the Waterfall trap. 
### Repairable Anatomy:
Design for "plastic surgery." Components should be modular enough to be repaired or replaced without compromising the core skeleton.
